WEBSITE DESIGN INSPIRATIONS

Research One:

Clickable Image Slider

Linked to the corresponding section

What I am referring to as a clickable image slider is the image slider which can be clicked and after the click, the section corresponding to what the image was telling us is opened.

For implementing a clickable image slider, I studied the Image Slider JS code provided by the respected teachers from our module, and then wrote the same kind of code for my clickable image slider with my original modification.

I used for loop, getElementById and innerHTML for the whole thing with anchor tag and img tag.

Although, manual navigation is missing in my product but I have included a hover effect in my image slider.

Research Two:

Center Alignment in the Home Page

Simple and Focused Look

In the Home Page of the Apple Official Website, the contents are aligned in the center which is giving it a good simple look with not many items showing up at once but items showing up one by one in a simple manner by aligning in the center giving a kind of ease to our eyes.

I implemented the same kind of plan to align the contents in my Home Page in the center as Home Page is generally the first page to visit and the visitor will not be fed up with numerous contents at once but the visitor will be treated with my contents in a easy view with ease. Plus, our eyes generally focus what's in the center.

Research Three:

Button Highlight Effect in Navbar

The highlight effect when we hover over the navigation bar buttons

I implemented the highlight when hover effect in my navbar. I used CSS for making the hover effect happen. I used :hover selector and changed the background-color and color properties accordingly and it worked perfectly.

Research Five:

Price Font Formatting

Simple and Focused Look

The color of the Price is jolly and bright which is a good color to keep in the price as it attracts the customer. The line-through in the Discounted Price is also a cool feature and I have tried to implement both as my product is also an E-Commerce Website.
